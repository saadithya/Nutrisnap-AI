How It Works

  1. Capture or Upload: Take a photo of your food or select an existing image from your gallery
  2. AI Analysis: Our advanced AI processes the image and identifies the food item
  3. Nutrition Data: Receive comprehensive nutritional information including macronutrients and micronutrients
  4. Review & Save: Verify the information and save it to your nutrition history
  5. Track Progress: Monitor your daily intake and work toward your health goals

Supported AI Models

  • Google Gemini Flash 2.5: Advanced multimodal AI for accurate food recognition and analysis
  • OpenAI GPT-4 Vision: Cutting-edge image analysis technology for comprehensive nutrition data

App Permissions

For optimal functionality, NutriSnap AI requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: To capture photos of food items for AI analysis
  • Photo Library: To access existing images for nutrition analysis
  • Internet: To communicate with AI services and retrieve nutritional data
  • Storage: To save user data, preferences, and nutrition history locally

All permissions are used solely for the core functionality of the app and your privacy is our top priority.

Technical Specifications

  • Platform: Android (API 21+)
  • Architecture: React Native with Expo
  • AI Integration: Google Gemini API and OpenAI API
  • Data Storage: Local storage with optional cloud sync
  • Image Processing: Base64 encoding for API communication
